### What are wireless headphones with microphones?

Wireless headphones with microphones are audio devices designed to provide hands-free listening and calling experience. They use Bluetooth technology to connect to smartphones or other multimedia devices without the need for wired connections. These headphones come with built-in microphones, allowing users to make phone calls, record audio, and use voice commands with virtual assistants like Siri or Google Assistant. 


### How do wireless headphones with microphones work?
