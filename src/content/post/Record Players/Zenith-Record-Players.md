Manual turntables are more traditional, with the user manually lowering and lifting the tonearm. They can provide a more precise and deliberate tracking, but require more user involvement. Automatic turntables, on the other hand, have a motor that will drop the needle and lift it once the record is finished, providing a more convenient and hassle-free experience.

### How do I maintain and care for my Zenith record player?
