### 2. Motor Type

Technics record players typically come in two motor types: direct-drive and belt-drive. Direct-drive turntables are known for their precision and responsiveness, allowing for more accurate sound reproduction. However, they can be more expensive and may require more occasional maintenance. 

Belt-drive record players are generally more affordable and feature a smooth operation, thanks to the rubber belt that powers the turntable. Although they're not as precise as direct-drive systems, they offer a more consistent playback experience for most users.
